About Us

Solaris Medicare Diagnostic Center, is at the forefront of medical technology and expertise. We are bringing the most advanced and state of art diagnostic facilities, under one roof and delivering these healthcare services in a patient friendly environment at very affordable prices. We seek to treating all patients with compassion, kindness and provide healthcare services of outstanding quality at a very reasonable cost. Our endeavour in the years to come is to ensure that Solaris Medicare Diagnostic Center emerges as a Center of excellence for affordable diagnostic facilities to all sections of society, so that patients do not have to travel to bigger cities for advanced treatment options.
